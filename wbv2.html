FlambMail - What is WBv2?

WBv2 stands for Whiteboard version 2. Originally, FlambMail was written on a whiteboard - humble beginnings. After the creator got bored of writing mail on a whiteboard, he/she didn't write mail at all. But that's fine, because later, the creator released WBv2. New and improved, and the mail would refresh daily. How interesting!
